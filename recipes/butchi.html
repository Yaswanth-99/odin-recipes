Butter Chicken

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish consisting of some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, fish) or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Cardamom
  • Meat
  • Rice
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Add the spices and cook on low flame
  3. Marinate meat
  4. Add meat into the rice and cook for sometime.
